Bond 11 Plus English

Our Bond 11 Plus products provide a range of structured book resources to improve your child's core English skills from age 5 through to age 13.

The 11 Plus English tests cover reading and writing, testing literacy skills which children need to understand and use the English language.

English exams may differ from region to region, but a paper will generally consist of :

  • Comprehension exercises, where children have to read pieces of text and answer questions on them
  • Writing tasks, where children have to write something themselves
  • Punctuation and Grammar problems, where children show their understanding of how English works as a language
  • Spelling tasks, where children spot incorrect spellings

The Bond 11 Plus English resources provide clear guidance on key knowledge and skills every child needs, comprehensive practice and revision materials as well as essential advice on exam technique.
