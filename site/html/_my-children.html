My Children

Here you can create a profile for each of your children. They will then be able to log into their own Bond Online account and do their tests. You will be able to see their progress by clicking on View reports. Not sure what to do? We offer a range of help options:

  • Watch a video tutorial
  • Or take a look at our step-by-step guide to finding the tests
