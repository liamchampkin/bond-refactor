A quick step-by-step guide to finding the tests.

Follow these three simple steps and get your child practising on Bond Online.

Step one: buy a subscription.

To access the online tests you must first go to our shop and buy a subscription for your child.

  1. Click the shop link in the red bar at the top of the page.


  2. Select the Add to basket button next to the type of subscription that you want to buy (either monthly or annual payment).
  3. Click the Go to Checkout button.


  4. Now you are in the checkout page, review your order details. Then click the New button to add a billing address.
  5. Tick the box to agree to our terms and conditions (once you have read them).
  6. Then click the Continue to payment details button.


  7. You will be taken to a secure payment processing page. Add your card details to complete your order

If your payment is successful you will recieve a confirmation email.

Step two: create a profile for your child.

Once you have purchased a subscription you must then set up a profile for your child.

  1. Go to your My Children page.
  2. Click the Create a learner profile button.


  3. Add your child's details
  4. Click the Create a profile button.

Your child can now log in and start taking the tests.

Step three: login to your child profile.

The tests are accessed by logging on with your child's profile details.

  1. First logout of your parent account.


  2. Click either of the green Login buttons.


  3. Then enter the username and password for the profile that you set up for your child.


  4. Click on a subject name to find the tests.

If you have any further questions on how to access the tests take a look at our FAQ pages.
